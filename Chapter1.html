Chapter I

When Shailly Met Nimisha

If someone had told me that a debate would introduce me to one of the most important people in my life...

I'd probably have laughed.

Because honestly... our first proper conversation wasn't sweet. It wasn't emotional. It was a debate.

Neither of us knew it then...
but this was the beginning.

Funny how life works.

We rarely realize that an ordinary day...

is quietly becoming a memory we'll carry for years.

Looking back...

I don't remember who won the debate.

But I know I did

because I found YOU!
